How To Play

Guess the word in 6 tries.

  • Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word.
  • The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was.

Hard Mode

Toggle Hard Mode using the switch in the top-right corner of the screen (next to the 🔄 button). When enabled:

  • Green letters must stay in the same position in your next guess.
  • Yellow letters must appear somewhere in your next guess.
  • Hard Mode can only be turned on before your first guess of each round.

A new word is available each day!
